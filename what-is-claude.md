# 🟡 What is claude

* It's an AI assistant designed to be your thinking partner



**Capabilities:**

* Writing and content creation:
* Research and analysis:
  * Claude's large context window, which can ingest 200K+ tokens (about 500 pages of text or more), allowing Claude to consider extensive materials in a single conversation
* Coding assistance:
* Problem-solving and reasoning:
* Learning new things:
  * Learning mode is a new Claude experience that guides your reasoning process rather than providing answers, helping develop critical thinking skills



**Ways to access:**

* Claude.ai (and the associated mobile and desktop apps)
* Claude Code: Agentic coding tool
* Claude and slack: directly into your team's communication tool
* Claude for excel
