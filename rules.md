# Rules

* **Rules** are standing instructions you give the AI — applied automatically, every time, for every prompt
* You write them once; the agent reads and follows them without you repeating yourself

**Types:**

1. **Project Rules:**

* Local
* Written as `.mdc` files
* Stored in the `.cursor/rules/`&#x20;

2. **Global Rules:**

* Personal
* Settings ⇒ Rules
* Apply across all your projects on your machine

3. **Team Rules:**

* Defined in the Cursor Dashboard by admins
* Automatically distributed to all team members via the cloud
