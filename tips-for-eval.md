# ✈️ Tips for Eval

* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Quick and dirty is ok to start**</mark>
* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**As you find places where your eval fails to capture human judgement as to what system is better, use that as an opportunity to improve the metric**</mark>
* <mark style="color:purple;background-color:purple;">**Look for places where performance is worse than humans**</mark>
